Instruction For Online Hackathon

In this unresting scenario of 2020 due to the COVID 19 spread which has yet not found any cure. We set up a platform and call all the young minds of the nation to come up with ideas to curb and cure the CORONA pandemic.HACK FOR COVID 19 a hackathon presented by Idea Innovation Cell, VEER SURENDRA SAI UNIVESITY OF TECHNOLOGY. Participate and exhibit your ideas to overcome this havoc in 3 rounds.




ROUND1:




Submit an abstract explaining about your solution within 250 words.

Deadline - 1st April till 11:59pm.



ROUND2:




Submit a PowerPoint presentation explaining details of the solution along with its pros and eases.

Deadline - 5th April till 11:59 pm.



ROUND3:




Pitch your solution to the panel on ZOOM.

On 11th of April.



Race your minds as innovation has always come from the unexpected so believe in yourself and strive to defeat this annihilator and file in your contribution to help save the world against COVID 19

Along with that win EXCITING PRIZES and GOODIES.
Isolated in our homes yet United by our minds. Be a pillar in the set back from the CORONA Outbreak
